Job Description - Android Developer

We are looking for an experienced Android Developer to join our mobile development team. As an Android Developer, you will be responsible for designing and developing high-quality mobile applications for the Android platform. If you have a passion for mobile technologies and a strong background in Android development, we'd love to hear from you.

Responsibilities:

  • Design and develop advanced applications for the Android platform
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define project requirements
  • Ensure the performance, quality, and responsiveness of applications
  • Identify and fix bugs and performance bottlenecks
  • Maintain code quality and ensure best practices are followed

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or a related field
  • Proven experience in Android application development
  • Strong knowledge of Android SDK, Java, and Kotlin
  • Familiarity with RESTful APIs and web services
  • Experience with version control systems like Git
  • Good understanding of software development methodologies and practices
